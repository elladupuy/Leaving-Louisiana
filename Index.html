Despite this website being about leaving Louisiana I cannot go without acknowledging the fear I have of leaving and what keeps me here. While I think our systems are completely flawed and not meant to help people escape poverty or improve our society or overall quality of life, I can admit that I have been able to take advantage of the opportunities given to me.
Growing up my parents struggled with substance abuse and poverty throughout my childhood which left me at a huge disadvantage in school and participating in extracurricular activities. It was only through the help of my close knit community that i was able to get through school. My friend's parents made sure I had rides to practice and paid for any fees. I was able to find my passion for art through a talented art program my school hosted and I was able to apply for financial aid, state testing and college through the help of attentive advisors.
My friends helped me get a job which i still work for to this day, and the community I found at this job helped me join their marketing team that I work for now, which would have been otherwise impossible with my lack of experience
